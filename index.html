About Me

I am a self-taught 3D designer/artist currently exploring the depths of Computer Programming. I befriended Computers playing games on my good ol' laptop, doodling on MS-Paint and making things bounce on Scratch.

Then I moved to Blender on it's V2.76 to embark on a creative journey and now, it has been over 5 years and I have done numerous 3D projects and artworks. It's fun building and shaping ideas.

Among the many things I am interested in, I am currently focusing on mastering Python and cross-platform development using Flutter, with a plan to make greater use of my skillset in impactful creations and applications.
